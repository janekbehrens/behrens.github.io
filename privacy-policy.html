2. Hosting via GitHub Pages

This website is hosted by GitHub Pages (GitHub Inc., 88 Colin P Kelly Jr St, San Francisco, CA 94107, USA). When you visit the website, GitHub automatically collects server log data, including:

  • IP address of the requesting device
  • Date and time of the request
  • Requested URL
  • HTTP status code and bytes transferred

This data is collected by GitHub as technically necessary server logs and is outside our control. GitHub may transfer this data to the United States. GitHub is certified under the EU-US Data Privacy Framework.

Legal basis: Art. 6(1)(f) GDPR — legitimate interest in the technically sound provision of this website.
For more information, see the GitHub Privacy Statement.

3. Contact via Email

If you contact us using the email address provided on this website, the data you submit (your email address, optionally your name, and the content of your message) will be processed and stored for the purpose of handling your enquiry.

Legal basis: Art. 6(1)(b) GDPR (pre-contractual or contractual obligation) where your enquiry relates to a contract — otherwise Art. 6(1)(f) GDPR (legitimate interest in responding to enquiries).

Data will be deleted once your enquiry has been fully resolved and no statutory retention obligation applies.

4. Cookies and Tracking

This website uses no cookies, no web tracking, no analytics tools (e.g. Google Analytics), and no embedded third-party services (e.g. social media buttons, externally hosted fonts). No user profiles are created.

5. Your Rights

Under GDPR, you have the following rights against the data controller:

  • Access (Art. 15 GDPR): Information about what data is processed about you.
  • Rectification (Art. 16 GDPR): Correction of inaccurate data.
  • Erasure (Art. 17 GDPR): Deletion of your data.
  • Restriction of processing (Art. 18 GDPR).
  • Objection (Art. 21 GDPR): Against processing based on legitimate interests.
  • Data portability (Art. 20 GDPR).

To exercise your rights, contact us at: support@janekbehrens.de
