2. Description of the App

Priority Scoring for Jira is a Jira Cloud app that helps teams prioritise their backlog using structured scoring frameworks. The App provides:

  • Three custom field types on Jira issues: RICE score (Reach × Impact × Confidence ÷ Effort), WSJF score (Business Value + Time Criticality + Risk Reduction ÷ Job Size), and ICE score (Impact × Confidence × Ease).
  • A global dashboard page for project-level priority ranking and backlog health analysis.
  • A dashboard gadget showing ranked priority scores across a project's issues.
  • A Rovo AI agent ("Priority Scoring") that can suggest and — depending on the configured apply mode — apply RICE, WSJF, or ICE scores by analysing Jira issue content.
