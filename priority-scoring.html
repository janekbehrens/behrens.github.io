Scoring frameworks

Pick a framework — or define your own

RICE

RICE Score

Reach · Impact · Confidence · Effort

Balances customer reach and impact against confidence and effort. Best for product teams prioritizing feature work across a large user base.

(Reach × Impact × Confidence) ÷ Effort
WSJF

WSJF Score

Weighted Shortest Job First

Prioritizes items with the highest cost of delay relative to job size. Preferred by SAFe teams and engineering-led organizations.

Cost of Delay ÷ Job Duration
ICE

ICE Score

Impact · Confidence · Ease

Lightweight and fast. Great for growth teams, experiments, and quick prioritization without heavy data requirements.

Impact × Confidence × Ease
